About Me

Hi, I'm Emma Richardson

Writer, teacher, and mindfulness practitioner exploring the intersection of creativity and conscious living.

I started Mindful Musings in 2023 as a way to document my own journey toward a more intentional life. What began as a personal project quickly grew into a community of like-minded individuals seeking balance in an increasingly chaotic world.

With a background in creative writing and over a decade of meditation practice, I bring both artistic and contemplative perspectives to topics ranging from daily habits to transformative travel experiences.

When I'm not writing or teaching at the local community college, you'll find me hiking Pacific Northwest trails, experimenting with plant-based cooking, or curled up with a good book and my rescue cat, Miso.
